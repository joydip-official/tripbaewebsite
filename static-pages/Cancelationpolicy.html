Cancellation Policy

A. Cancellation by the Customer

Customers may cancel their bookings. Refund eligibility depends on the time of cancellation before departure:

  • More than 7 days before the departure date: 90% refund of the total tour cost.
  • Between 3 to 6 days before the departure date: 60% refund of the total tour cost.
  • Between 1 to 3 days before the departure date: 30% refund of the total tour cost.
  • Less than 24 hours before departure: No refund will be issued.

B. Cancellation by Tripbae

Tripbae reserves the right to cancel any tour due to unforeseen or unavoidable circumstances including, but not limited to:

  • Natural calamities
  • Political unrest
  • Insufficient participation

In such cases, customers will be offered the option of a full refund or alternate tour of equivalent value.

C. Changes Initiated by Tripbae

Minor itinerary changes may occur due to operational reasons. No compensation will be issued for such modifications.

For significant changes, customers will be notified in advance and may choose to:

  • Accept the changes
  • Opt for an alternate tour
  • Request a full refund

D. Non-Utilization of Services

No refund will be provided for unused services or missed days during the tour, regardless of the cause.

E. Delays, Health & Other Circumstances

No refund will be given for missed departures, illnesses, or personal emergencies.

Tripbae shall not be held liable for additional expenses arising from delays by airlines, buses, or other service providers.

F. Booking Transfers

Customers may transfer their booking to another person, subject to type of destination, prior approval and an administrative fee.

This request must be made at least 48 hours before the trip.

G. Third-Party Services

Refunds for bookings involving third-party providers (e.g., airlines, hotels) will follow the respective provider's cancellation and refund policies.
