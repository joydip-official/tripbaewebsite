Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

At Tripbae, we prioritize your privacy and are dedicated to safeguarding your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and disclose your information in the course of providing our travel and trekking services.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following personal details:

  • Full name, address, email ID, and contact number.
  • Emergency contact information.
  • Medical history or dietary preferences relevant to trip arrangements.
  • Passport details (for international treks).
  • Payment and transaction details.
  • Any additional information voluntarily provided during inquiry or booking.

3. Purpose of Data Collection

Your information is used for the following purposes:

  • To facilitate bookings and organize treks efficiently.
  • To communicate essential updates or information related to your trip.
  • To enhance our services based on user feedback and requirements.
  • To share promotional materials, only if you have opted in.

4. Sharing of Personal Information

We may disclose your information to:

  • Third-party service providers such as accommodation, transport, and insurance providers necessary for fulfilling our services.
  • Legal authorities, if mandated under applicable laws.

5. Data Security and Retention

Your data is stored on secure servers with standard encryption, firewalls, and restricted access. We retain personal information only for as long as it serves its intended purpose or as required by applicable law.

6. Access and Correction Rights

You may request access to or correction of your personal data by contacting us directly. We will process such requests in accordance with applicable laws.

7. Opt-Out Option

To unsubscribe from our promotional communications, you may use the opt-out link provided in our emails or contact us directly.

8. Use of Cookies and Tracking Tools

Our website may utilize cookies and similar technologies to enhance your browsing experience and collect analytical data.

9. External Website Links

Our platform may contain links to external websites. We are not responsible for the privacy policies or content of such third-party sites and advise users to review their respective policies.

10. Changes to the Policy

This Privacy Policy may be revised periodically to reflect updates in legal or operational practices. Users are encouraged to review this policy from time to time.
