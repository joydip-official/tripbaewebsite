Refund Policy

1. Refund Policy

A. Refund Process

  • Approved refunds will be processed within 7 working days from the date of cancellation request.
  • Refunds will be credited to the original payment method (bank account, credit card, UPI, etc.).

B. Late or Missing Refunds

  • Re-check your bank account.
  • Contact your credit card company—it may take time before the refund reflects.
  • Consult your bank, as posting delays are common.
  • If unresolved, contact us at support@tripbae.com.

2. Claims and Disputes

All claims or service-related complaints must be submitted in writing to hello@tripbae.com within 10 days after tour completion.

No claims will be entertained beyond this period.

3. Policy Updates

This Cancellation and Refund Policy is subject to change without prior notice. We recommend reviewing the latest policy at the time of booking.
